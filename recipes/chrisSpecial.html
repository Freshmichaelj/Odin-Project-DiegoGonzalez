Odin Recipes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour for coating
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 medium skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded 1/4 inch thick
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • ½ cup Marsala wine
  • ¼ cup cooking sherry

Directions

Step 1: Gather all Ingredients
Step 2: In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano.
Step 3: Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
Step 4: In a large skillet, melt butter in olive oil over medium heat. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown.
Step 5: Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms. Pour in wine and sherry.
Step 6: Cover skillet; simmer chicken 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy!
